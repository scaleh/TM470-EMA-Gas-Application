Welcome to the GAS Application

The Green Assessment and Screening or GAS application is designed for users who work with hazardous materials and chemicals. The application is able to show the material safety datasheet by either searching by inputting text, selecting or scanning the bar code of the hazardous material or chemical. From this, the user can use this information to perform a risk assessment of the work area or activity and therefore, ensure the health and safety for all participants. Additionally, the application is able to show the user on an interactive map where the nearest Personal Protective Equipment provider is based and also where local medical facilities are, in case of emergencies.

Disclaimer. Only trained personal are allowed to handle hazardous material when an adequate risk assessment has been performed in accordance with the COSHH Regulations 2002 (in the United Kingdom) and any other similar regulations world wide. Please also note that the MSDS content within the application remains the property of the manufacturer. Please contact the manufacturer directly for any safety concerns with any of the hazardous material noted. All locations of are correct as of September 2020.
